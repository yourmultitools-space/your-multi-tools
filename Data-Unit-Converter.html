About Data Units

Data storage units are used to measure digital information. The basic unit is the byte, and each subsequent unit is typically 1024 times larger than the previous one (in binary system).

Here's a quick reference for data unit conversions:

Unit Equivalent Bytes
1 Byte 8 bits 1
1 Kilobyte (KB) 1024 Bytes 1,024
1 Megabyte (MB) 1024 Kilobytes 1,048,576
1 Gigabyte (GB) 1024 Megabytes 1,073,741,824
1 Terabyte (TB) 1024 Gigabytes 1,099,511,627,776

1 Exabyte (EB) 1024 Petabytes 1,152,921,504,606,846,976
