Frequently Asked Questions

How does video compression work?
Video compression works by removing redundant information from the video file. Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality as much as possible. It analyzes each frame and removes data that isn't essential for human perception.
Will compressing a video reduce its quality?
Some quality loss is inevitable with compression, but our tool is designed to minimize this. Using the "High Quality" option will result in minimal quality loss, while "High Compression" will reduce file size more significantly but with more noticeable quality reduction.
What video formats are supported?
Our video compressor supports a wide range of formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WMV, FLV, and WebM. You can also convert between these formats during compression.
Is there a file size limit for compression?
Yes, our free version supports videos up to 500MB. For larger files, you may need to use our premium service or split the video into smaller segments before compression.
How long does compression take?
Compression time depends on several factors including file size, resolution, and the selected compression level. Typically, a 5-minute video takes 1-3 minutes to compress. Larger files or higher quality settings will take longer.
