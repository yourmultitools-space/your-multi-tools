Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website www.yourmultitools.space.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Your Multi Tools, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We want to make it clear that we do not collect any personal data or user information when you use our website. Any temporary data that may be generated during your session is automatically deleted and not stored.

Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike many websites, we do not collect any of the following information:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you access or use our website. We do not collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on our site, or any other usage statistics.
  • Cookies: We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to track your activity on our website.
  • Files and Data: Any files you upload or process using our tools are not stored on our servers after your session ends. All data is automatically deleted.

How We Do Not Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not use your information for any purpose. This means:

  • We do not use your data to provide or maintain our services
  • We do not send you notifications or marketing materials
  • We do not analyze user behavior to improve our services
  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling

Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not share any information with third parties. We do not share your data with service providers, business partners, affiliates, or any other entities because we simply do not have any data to share.

Data Security

Because we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. Any temporary data that may be generated during your use of our tools is automatically deleted when your session ends or immediately after processing.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, you do not need to exercise any data protection rights with us. There is no data to access, correct, update, or delete because we do not store any information about you.

Children's Privacy

Our website does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children because we do not collect any personal information at all.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

While we do not anticipate significant changes to this Privacy Policy since we do not collect data, we may update it from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this Privacy Policy.
